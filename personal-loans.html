The Application Process

Getting a personal loan through Horizon Financial Services is straightforward:

  • Step 1: Complete our simple online application in just minutes
  • Step 2: We perform a soft credit check to find matching lenders
  • Step 3: Review multiple loan offers and choose the best one for you
  • Step 4: Complete the lender's application and provide verification
  • Step 5: Receive your funds, typically within 1-2 business days

Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a personal loan, you typically need to be at least 18 years old, have a valid bank account, provide proof of income, and meet the lender's credit requirements. The best rates are reserved for applicants with excellent credit, but we work with lenders who serve a wide range of credit profiles.
