Debt Settlement Services

A proven alternative to bankruptcy with potential savings on enrolled debts

Resolve Your Debt for Less Than You Owe

Debt settlement is a strategic approach to resolving overwhelming debt by negotiating with creditors to accept less than the full amount owed. This solution can provide significant relief for individuals struggling with unsecured debts like credit cards, medical bills, and personal loans.

Proven Results

Based on past results, individuals who enroll in our debt settlement program and remain through graduation see savings on their enrolled debts of approximately 40% before fees, or 22% when program fees are included.

How Debt Settlement Works

  • Evaluation: We assess your financial situation and determine if debt settlement is right for you
  • Enrollment: Eligible debts are enrolled in the program with customized payment plans
  • Savings Account: You make monthly deposits into a dedicated savings account
  • Negotiation: Our team negotiates settlements with your creditors
  • Settlement: Once agreements are reached, settlements are paid from your account
  • Resolution: Enrolled debts are resolved, typically in 24-48 months

Benefits of Debt Settlement

  • Potentially reduce your total debt by 40% or more before fees
  • More affordable than many credit counseling programs
  • Avoid bankruptcy and its long-term consequences
  • Flexible payment schedules based on your budget
  • Single monthly payment into your dedicated account
  • Professional negotiation with creditors on your behalf
  • Path to becoming debt-free in 2-4 years

Types of Debt We Can Settle

  • Credit card debt
  • Medical bills and healthcare expenses
  • Personal loans (unsecured)
  • Department store credit accounts
  • Collection agency accounts
  • Repossession deficiency balances

Important Considerations

Debt settlement may impact your credit score during the program. However, for many people already struggling with debt, the benefit of resolving overwhelming obligations outweighs the temporary credit impact. Not all debts are eligible, and not all clients complete the program. Results vary based on individual circumstances.

Why Choose Our Debt Settlement Program?

  • Experienced negotiators with proven track record
  • Transparent fee structure with no hidden costs
  • Personalized program tailored to your situation
  • Regular updates on settlement progress
  • Dedicated support team throughout the process
  • No upfront fees - you only pay when debts are settled

Is Debt Settlement Right for You?

Debt settlement may be a good option if you:

  • Have significant unsecured debt ($10,000 or more)
  • Are struggling to make minimum payments
  • Want to avoid bankruptcy
  • Have experienced financial hardship
  • Are committed to resolving your debt
  • Can make consistent monthly payments into a savings account
